About Us

Heavenly Delights Bakeshop was founded in 2010 by Jane Smith with the mission to provide the freshest and most delicious baked goods in town. I am so excited to announce the opening of Heavenly Delight's, a new bakery here in Angeles City, 03! Our cakes come in all shapes, sizes, and flavors. Our cupcakes are light and fluffy, made with the freshest of ingredients, and come in all sorts of delicious flavors.

We have something for everyone at Heavenly Delight's, so come and check us out! Come to Heavenly Delight's for all your baking needs.
